Instructions

There will be balloons appearing on your screen. They will be moving on your screen(left, right, up and down).
Tap on the shoot button to shoot a dart. The button is on the right side of your screen.(for computer, this button would be spacebar)
Use the Joystick at the left side of your screen to aim your dart at the balloon. There will be a translucent red line showing you where you are aiming.
For computer users, use your mouse to aim at the balloons.
Different Balloons will give different amounts of points. eg : red = 5, purple = 10. The points that the balloon holds, will be shown on the balloon itself.
Your total points will be at the top of the screen.

Good Luck, Have Fun!


The back button is at the top right corner of your screen, Press it to go back to the main menu.
